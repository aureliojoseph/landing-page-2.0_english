About Me

As a software developer with almost 2 years of experience, I have a solid foundation in building responsive websites and web applications. My specialty is in VueJs, where I have 9 months of immersive experience, but I am also proficient in JavaScript, ReactJs, TypeScript, SASS, HTML5, and CSS3. I have experience with component development following Design System Patterns, flow and screen development according to Design specifications using Figma, and applying the Style Guide for VueJs and the B.E.M. methodology. I have used libraries such as Element Plus, Phosphor Icons, Ion Icons, Tailwind CSS, Material Icons, and Bootstrap.

My experience collaborating with Frontend and Backend, Java, Ruby, Quality Assurance, Design (UI/UX), and Integration teams has given me an overview of the entire development process, where I can learn and contribute at each stage. I have experience working with Git, Github, and Bitbucket, and project management based on agile methodologies and using tools like Slack and JIRA.

Prior to my career in software development, I worked in Business Management, where I helped people improve their skills and companies achieve their goals. In 2011, I started a retail franchise Business where I coordinated financial and administrative areas, managing 3 stores. My background as a Manager and Consultant has taught me the importance of solid Strategic Planning, which is key to inspiring people with an authentic sense of belonging to organizations and delivering a genuine customer experience that transforms the performance of the company and the product.

In 2021, I made my career transition to the world of programming, which started as a curiosity and turned into an extraordinary and fascinating universe that I am passionate about. As a developer, my goal is to develop scalable, high-quality products with an excellent user experience that impacts the lives of thousands of people. I am a team player and enjoy working collaboratively, contributing my expertise to the team. I am excited to collaborate with professionals who value excellence, innovation, and teamwork.
